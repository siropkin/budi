## Key insight from this session

**The heuristic pipeline is the right approach.** The reranker and embedding research both show that the hand-tuned 5-channel fusion + intent-aware score floors + skip patterns is hard to beat with ML approaches at budi's data scale (951 pairs). The remaining regressions (9%) are structural (thin wrappers, HNSW variance, LLM variance) — not solvable by better scoring.
